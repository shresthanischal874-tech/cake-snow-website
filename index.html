Our Story

A little corner of warmth in Bhaktapur

Cake Snow was born from a simple belief: great coffee and freshly baked treats have the power to turn an ordinary day into something memorable.

Nestled in Anantalingeshwar, we craft every cake, every pastry, and every cup with care — using quality ingredients and recipes refined through passion, not shortcuts.

Whether you stop in for a quick espresso or settle in for an afternoon slice, you'll always leave a little happier than when you arrived.
